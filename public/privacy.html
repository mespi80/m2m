2. Information We Collect

The types of information we collect depend on how you use the Services. We may collect information in the following categories:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account information. When you create an account, we may collect information such as your name, email address, password, country of residence, preferred language, and other registration details.
  • Profile information. You may choose to add details to your profile, such as a profile photo, age or date of birth, gender, height, weight, typical training volume, preferred sport(s), and training goals.
  • Training preferences and settings. We collect information you share about your current fitness level, race targets, available training days, training zones, perceived effort, injury status, and any constraints that influence your training plan.
  • Communications with us. If you contact us (for example, via email or in-app support), we collect the content of your message, your contact details, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Feedback and surveys. We may collect information when you respond to surveys, submit reviews, or provide feedback about the Services.
  • Payment and subscription information. When you purchase a subscription or other paid offering, we collect information related to the transaction (such as subscription tier, billing address, and partial payment card details). Full payment information is processed by our third-party payment providers in accordance with their own policies.

2.2 Information We Collect When You Use the Services

  • Activity and training data. When you connect third-party accounts (for example, Garmin Connect) or manually log activities, we collect information about your endurance sessions and related metrics, such as:
    • Date and time of the activity
    • Distance, duration, pace, speed, cadence, power output, and similar performance statistics
    • Heart rate and other physiological signals, if available
    • Elevation, GPS routes, and related location data (if enabled)
    • Device type and sport mode used (e.g., run, ride, swim)
  • Generated metrics and insights. Based on the data we collect, we may compute additional metrics such as training load, intensity, recovery estimates, predicted race times, and plan adherence, to support training-plan generation and monitoring.
  • Usage information. We collect information about how you interact with the Services, such as the pages you view, features you use, links you click, and the dates and times of your visits.
  • Device and technical information. We may automatically collect technical data from the device and browser you use to access the Services, including IP address, operating system, device identifiers, app version, language settings, and crash logs.
  • Location information. With your consent, we may collect approximate or precise location data from your device to support certain features (for example, adjusting training recommendations by time zone or local conditions). You can control location access via your device settings.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies, web beacons, SDKs, and similar technologies to recognize you, remember your preferences, analyze how the Services are used, and deliver relevant content and marketing. For more details, see Section 7 (Cookies and Similar Technologies).

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

  • Connected devices and platforms. When you choose to connect M2Endurance with third-party platforms or devices (for example, Garmin Connect, other fitness platforms, or health apps), we receive information from those services in accordance with your permissions and their policies. This may include training histories, activity metrics, health-related data, and device information.
  • Authentication providers. If you sign up or log in using an account from another service (such as a social login or identity provider), we may receive profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Service providers and partners. We may receive information from analytics providers, marketing partners, and other vendors who help us operate, improve, and promote the Services.
